PreSwitcher

by Ryan Petrich & chpwn

Switch apps quickly and easily

Integrates with SpringBoard for a nice and smooth experience

Uses little memory and saves to disk when running low

Won't slow down your device -- hooks only whats needed

Palm Web OS inspired interface

Easy to learn gestures: flick to close, swipe to scroll, tap to open

Loads of settings: activation and interface

Custom backgrounds are supported via WinterBoard themes, soon to be able to theme even more
